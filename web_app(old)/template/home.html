Overview

In todays time we see a lot of the shortage of the doctors in the world especially in India.A lot of people are suffering a lot without the help of the proper medical checkup. Also most of the cases many cases arise leading to death due to lack of timely medical checkup

Application

  • To help people avoid paying huge amount to the doctors unnecessarily
  • To extend the role of the technology in the medical field
